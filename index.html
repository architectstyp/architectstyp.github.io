Zero Carbon

Every joule of energy put into these buildings is meaningful and offset.
We study the Whole Site Life Cycle Assessment to make our buildings efficient, from start to forever.

We need to build better, now. Climate change is happening and the building and construction sector has been producing too many emissions, pollutants, and waste for too long.

Embodied carbon emissions from the building sector produce 11% of annual global greenhouse gas emissions.

The goal is a net-zero carbon footprint with net-zero off-site energy and operating emissions. We are meeting and exceeding the standards set by the globally recognized leader in sustainable building, the International Living Future Institute. Every building we build will stand up to their two measures: Zero Energy certification and Zero Carbon certification. We also meet and exceed the criteria for the US Department of Energy’s Zero Energy Ready homes, and the US Environmental Protection Agency’s Energy Star Homes. We utilize Watersense program products, perform HERS tests to ensure a very airtight home (think energy efficient).

We build homes that can last 500 years, so that all the energy we put in to the building, is fully utilized throughout its lifetime. As a Whole Site Life Cycle Assessment, we assess the carbon footprint of every material, including every foot it travels and every bit of energy in its production. We then balance the equation of your site + construction + your home, to give you a net-zero carbon footprint. We embrace on-site renewable energy like solar panels so that the all the energy you produce is all the energy your home needs to run.

We build homes that reduce construction waste. We design to best utilize standard material dimensions, and our exterior and interior walls contain the fewest and most highly efficient layers possible. Our structural system allows the exterior shell to be completely independent from the interior walls, which also means that your home’s 500 year lifespan can easily have many iterations.

We build homes that reduce pollutants and eliminate toxins. All of our building materials are certified by Declare Labels and are free from the Red List. We use formaldehyde-free and low/no-VOC products throughout your home, whether it’s something you’ll touch everyday or never see.

We help people make their existing homes better. Whether it’s a warmer “winter jacket,” or better summer shading that you desire, an update to your energy-consuming appliances, or just some help with laying things out, we are happy to assist you with any project, large or small.
